How to use Gothic Damage Calculator

  1. Use radio button to choose between Gothic 1 and Gothic 2 (choosing Gothic 2 will provide accurate results for both vanilla and Night of the Raven expansion).
  2. Using spinners, input all relevant information about the Nameless Hero, his weapon and the target, you are trying to hit.
    • Only spinners with blue border and green background are 'relevant'. Other spinners will not affect the results.
  3. Click on the 'Run' button to see the results.

Using spinners:

  1. You can choose to either input the number directly into the input field, or use the up/down buttons to increment/decrement the number.
  2. You can only use integers greater than or equal to zero, i.e. {0, 1, 2, ...}.
  3. Each spinner has defined minimum and maximum value:
    • 0–100: weapon skills, bonus weapon skill, lightning hit chance,
    • 0–5,000: strength, dexterity, weapon damage, lightning damage, melee armor, ranged armor,
    • 1–5,000: number of hits,
    • 1–1,000,000: hitpoints.
