Used formulas

Gothic 1

  • Melee weapons (1H or 2H):
    • normal damage = weapon damage + strength − melee armor
    • critical damage = 2 ⁎ weapon damage + strength − melee armor
  • Ranged weapons (bow or crossbow):
    • normal damage = weapon damage − ranged armor
    • critical damage = 2 ⁎ weapon damage − ranged armor

Gothic 2

  • Melee weapons (1H or 2H):
    • normal damage = (weapon damage + strength − melee armor − 1) / 10, not lower than 5
    • critical damage = weapon damage + strength − melee armor, not lower than 5
  • Ranged weapons (bow or crossbow):
    • weapon damage + dexterity − ranged armor, not lower than 5

Claw of Beliar's bonus lightning damage ignores armor.

Even if a weapon has strength/dexterity requiremets to equip, it still follows the formulas above, so the required attribute might not contribute to the damage output.

Formulas, which I used in Gothic Damage Calculator, were put together by WernerTWC from worldofplayers.de (World of Gothic) forum.

For more information follow the link to see WernerTWC's original post:
